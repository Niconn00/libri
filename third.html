About the Book

The Secret Garden tells the story of Mary Lennox, a spoiled and neglected girl who is sent to live with her uncle in a large, isolated manor in the English countryside after her parents' death. There, she discovers a hidden, overgrown garden and, with the help of new friends, begins to restore it to its former glory. Through this process, Mary undergoes a transformation, learning about nature, friendship, and the importance of kindness and compassion.
